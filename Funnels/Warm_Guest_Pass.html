How To Create Your Warm Guest Pass Landing Page:

The first webpage that you’ll want to create is a guest past page mostly for your warm traffic.

Why? Because warm traffic will be educated on what your event is about and all they need to do as the next step is book the call. Too much additional information (like the 🙋🏻 Cold Landing Page Event Registration webpage) can be overselling and confusing.

Two common sources of warm leads are people on your email list and DM marketing. Or let's say you have a friend or client you were talking or texting with and have enough rapport with them. That's also considered warm traffic.

Regarding people on your email list, you can "sell" the event in the body of your email, tell them all about it, get them all excited, and the call to action in that email is click here to get booked (see live event launch email templates).

One exception of sending your email list to the cold traffic page is to capture their mobile phone number so that you can call/text them if they do not book the call. If you don't plan on doing that, then you're better off directing them to this warm traffic page.

In DM marketing, the script (take IG for example) you take them through will be educating them on what content you'll be covering in your event and they have agreed they're interested in it, so you just send them straight to the booking page instead of the cold traffic page because you'll be overselling or confusing them.
