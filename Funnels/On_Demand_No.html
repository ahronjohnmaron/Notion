On Demand No Interview

In the 📺 On Demand With Interview version, guests are booking interviews and during your call, it’s determined the on demand videos are the way to go.

In this version, you’re allowing guests access to the training without having to do an interview. Instead, they just book a feedback call and they’ll be able to access the training.

We created this funnel as a downsell to those who weren’t willing to book/do an interview. This is also something you can follow up and offer to those who haven’t replied or who say they’re too busy to do an interview.

One downside though is that you’ll get more unqualified prospects on feedback calls since you weren’t able to talk to them on an interview beforehand.

This will NOT solve the issue of people in different time zones who can’t do an interview because they’ll have the same issue when booking the necessary feedback call prior to giving them access to the training. For this reason, at some point it’s worth building out a more traditional sales page for your program so that people can buy without having to talk to anyone at a certain time.

This funnel is probably not worth building unless you’re already getting 10 or so registrations per day. At that volume, it’ll be worth your time to have this option available to capture this “I don’t want to interview” segment of people. Otherwise, you’re likely better off investing the time into optimizing other areas of your business and save this until there are enough prospects in this situation where it justifies putting 5+ hours into building this.

As you can see, page 1 looks very much like the guest pass page, except some minor change in the text and you’re embedding a rolling 5-days ish in advance feedback call calendar for your enroller as that’s the amount of time you’d ideally want your prospect to finish watching the videos by. Also add as many testimonials as you can to the bottom of the page.

Integrations

What you’re doing is setting up the Calendly feedback call as the trigger to the automations. From there, you can put into the welcome email with the link to your training into that workflow email in Calendly or you can get fancy and zap them into a portal like Kajabi or HighLevel if you want the urgency of watching it before access goes away on the day of the feedback call.
