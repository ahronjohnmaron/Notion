What is the purpose of a Cold Traffic Landing Page?

The reason you have this cold traffic funnel is for the traffic where they don’t know you very well or what your event is about. Thus, you need a webpage that explains your credibility, what your virtual event is about, and the benefits of attending.

For example, let’s say you’re a guest on someone else’s podcast where you only have 30 seconds to pitch your virtual event. First of all, the audience just met you (through the host) so you only had that small amount of airtime for a new audience to get to know you. Secondly, when it came time to talk about your event, you’ll probably have limited time to promote it. It’s not like your own podcast where you can talk about it as much as you like. Based on these factors, you’ll probably want to use this cold traffic funnel.

Compare that scenario to the people who have been on your email list or have been following you for months on social media.
