Funnels

In marketing, we call “funnels” the different paths your prospects can do down to ultimately lead them into your virtual event and becoming a customer. Each funnel is built for different situations so that you’re able to give your prospects the best experience possible. Here is an overview for the different types of funnels and the general order for you to make them:

1.

Guest Pass to Interview - This lightweight page is framed as “you can get a free guest pass” to our next training. There are no details to the event. Just sign up for an interview call to attend the next training.

☀️
Warm Guest Pass to Interview
2.

Cold Landing Page - Free Registration to Interview - The first webpage to this funnel is a detailed breakdown of the event and why they should attend. They fill in their info to register and the next page they schedule their interview call.

🥶
Cold Landing Page Registration to Interview
3.

On Demand With Interview - This isn’t a webpage you send traffic to, but instead a webpage with video recordings of your 2-day event that you tell them about during the interview if they can’t attend an upcoming live event.

📺
On Demand With Interview
4.

Free Registration No Interview - At the backbone of all our funnels is the pre-event interview as part of the registration process because it significantly improves attendance among other benefits. However, there could be a situation where you have a huge following or anticipating a large amount of traffic where you won’t have the people or time to do scheduled interviews (though you can call as many of them after they register to do a more informal impromptu interview). Thus, in this more traditional funnel, they register and get the details (including ground rules) to attend without having to schedule an interview.

🙈
Registration No Interview
5.

Paid Registration No Interview - This is the same as above, except you’re charging for your 2-day virtual event (typically under $50-100).

6.

On Demand No Interview - In this on demand funnel, people can access the training simply by booking a feedback call (no interview necessary).

🙅🏻‍♀️
On Demand No Interview
