Offer

Your offer is the packaging of your products/services (what your customer gets when they work with you) so that it is something desirable and premium that your customers want to buy. You can use our VIP worksheet to guide you.

Your offer is composed of:

1.

Model (the triangle) - This shows the methodology/milestones that you take your clients through to achieve a particular end result.

2.

Tools - These are the deliverables and things they get, such as training videos, live classes, templates, graphics, files, resources, etc.

3.

Support - These are the different ways they can get help from you and your team whenever they have questions, are stuck, need clarity, etc.

4.

Pricing - This is how the investment works and how much it costs to join your program.
