Support

These are the different ways they can get help from you and your team whenever they have questions, are stuck, need clarity, etc. These are various methods you can provide support as ways to reach you or your team for help:

  • • Live trainings with Q&A
  • • Telegram group chat
  • • Facebook group
  • • Email
  • • Phone or texting - If you have a super premium VIP offer, this might be an option.
  • • Help desk / support ticket system - If you have a ton of volume, this might make sense, but it often times can feel impersonal.
  • • Knowledge base - Using something like Notion where you can document everything, it serves as a place where they can find the answers themselves.

If you need some ideas, refer to the VIP Worksheet PDF.
