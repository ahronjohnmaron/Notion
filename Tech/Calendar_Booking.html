Calendar Booking

So what will you need a calendar booking software for? You'll need it for the following reasons:

1.

The primary reason you'll be using it is for prospects to book pre-event interview calls as part of the registration process for your live event.

2.

But by making the live event (and enrollment call) an event in your calendar service, we can leverage their automated text and email reminders.

a. That means you don't need a CRM/EMS (email marketing service) if you don't have one. Your calendar service can send out the email reminders.
b. If you do have a CRM/EMS, you don't have to worry about your interviewer going into your CRM/EMS to tag the attendee as successfully approved or create an automation that does so.
c. You don't need to get a separate texting software to send text reminders, which are critical (and more effective than email) for reminding the attendees.
3.

There is no data entry error or forgetting by the attendee to input the event (and enrollment call) into their calendar. The calendar service can automatically create an event in their calendar for them.
