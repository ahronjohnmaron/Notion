Notion

Notion is great software you can use not only as a way to document processes in your business (making your own wiki), but it can also be used inexpensively as a client hub. Here are some videos that cover how to:

  • • Set up your client hub in Notion teamspace.
  • • Adding pages and subpages.
  • • Adding your clients as a guest to the page (and not the teamspace) so they can view and comment on your content. As of this writing, the free plan allows for up to 10 guests/clients, the $10/mo plan can accommodate 100, and the $18/mo plan can do 250.
  • • How to add content into your client hub - text, videos, images, emojis, and more.
