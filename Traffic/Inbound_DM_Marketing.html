Inbound DM Marketing

Make sure to check out the Inbound DM Marketing Training that goes along with this!

Attracting new followers often comes from your content, engaging actively with other accounts, or word-of-mouth recommendations. These are just a few examples of how followers can find you.

However, many entrepreneurs lack a clear process for engaging with new followers. This strategy outlines how to connect with your new followers, open a conversation, and lead them into your event.

If your content is already generating a steady stream of new followers, you can skip to Step 3 of this process.

If you’re not seeing many new followers yet, don’t worry. This strategy also includes actionable ways to actively engage with your target audience to attract inbound followers.

Steps to Create Inbound Leads Through Engagement:

Step 1: Identify Your Ideal Client

Who is your target audience? Get specific about the type of person you want to reach.

Step 2: Engage with Your Audience

Follow, comment on posts and stories, and engage actively with your target audience. You can do this manually, use software, or delegate it to a virtual assistant (VA).

Step 3: DM Each New Follower with These Scripts

1st Message:

“Hi [Name], thanks for the follow! By chance, have you heard about the free online class I’m running on [insert topic]?”

Follow-Up Message:

“Hey [Name], just following up on my last message. [Comment on something you noticed about them—e.g., a compliment about their profile or content.] I’d love to share more about my upcoming free training. I think it could be valuable to you! Would you like more details?”

If they say yes, send this:

“Hi [Name], great to connect with you! The workshop I’m hosting is all about [insert topic]. It’s completely free, and the process is super exciting—I think you’ll be impressed! Would you be interested in joining us?”

If they express interest, send the registration link:

“Fantastic, [Name]! Here’s the link to register and join us: [Insert link]. Let me know if you have any questions!”

Key Takeaway:

This approach helps turn new followers into potential event attendees by creating a clear and friendly engagement process. By identifying your ideal clients and starting conversations with value, you can grow your audience and connect them to your offerings effectively.

Auditing Your DM Messaging:

Is it something your target audience can relate to?

Is there a clear ROI in your bold promise?

Is the bold promise highly desirable?

Is the promise worth putting life on hold for two days?

Is the bold promise something they already want?
