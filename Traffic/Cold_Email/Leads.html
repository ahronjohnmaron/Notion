Leads

Getting Clear About Your ICP

ICP stands for Ideal Customer Profile. This means it’s important to have a clear ideal of who you are targeting. Cold email is most effective when you're targeting businesses (or someone who works for a business) and not individual consumers. The reason is simple: businesses and their team can be identified and found systematically by things like company, industry, and job title.

Example: Our ICP is coaches, consultants, and course creators. That makes it easy to search for job titles like "life coach," "business coach," or "relationship coach" and reliably find exactly the right people to reach out to. This will be important to keep in mind as we go through strategies to where to find them.

Stacking Lead Sources

Although leads from Instantly can keep you busy for some time, you do want to keep in mind that the long term strategy is to keep stacking different leads sources so that you have different places to find email addresses to load into your cold email marketing machine.

Imagine you have an orange juice company. You can work with one particular farmer to source your oranges for your factory to juice them, but over the long term you’ll want to keep adding on more farmers so that you have more orange sources in case they run out, can’t grow as quickly, you want better quality, etc. Same with sources for leads.
