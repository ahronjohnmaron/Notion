Cold Email

A Cost-Efficient Way to Reach Your Ideal Customers at Scale

A Practical Growth Channel for B2B (and B2C via Gatekeepers)

Cold email is one of the most reliable and scalable marketing channels, especially for B2B offers and high-ticket services.

We use this exact system in our own business, and it generates six figures per year in revenue from cold email alone.

It can also work for B2C when you target the businesses/gatekeepers who already control access to your audience. For example, if you target investors (B2C), you can email accountants or financial planners, which is B2B.

At the least (and additional strategy), you can use cold email to email podcasts hosts to ask to be a guest on their show.

Instead of relying on algorithms or inbound demand, cold email lets you proactively reach your exact ICP directly in a controlled, scalable way.

Why Cold Email Works

1) Highly Cost-Efficient Reach

With modern tools, you can send 15,000 emails per month to your target audience for under $200/month in infrastructure costs.

That makes cold email one of the lowest-cost ways to get your message in front of a large, qualified audience.

2) Direct Access to Decision Makers

No algorithms. No middlemen.

You’re putting your message directly into the inboxes of the exact people you want to work with: founders, partners, executives, and professionals with buying power.

3) Precise Targeting

You’re not broadcasting to a vague audience. Instead, you’re building lists based on specific criteria like:

  • Job title
  • Company size
  • Industry
  • Geography
  • Technology used
  • Buying intent

That means your outreach stays relevant and personalized.

4) Works for Both B2B and B2C (via Gatekeepers)

  • B2B: founders, CEOs, CMOs, partners, agency owners
  • B2C (indirect): professionals who already have your audience. Example: accountants → investors

5) Fully Scalable and Predictable

You can start small, validate your messaging, and then scale up to thousands of emails per month by adding inboxes without changing your core system.

How Cold Email Infrastructure Works

Sending thousands of emails per month from a single email address will quickly hurt deliverability and push your emails into spam.

The better approach is to use multiple email inboxes, each sending a small, safe number of emails per day. Together, they allow you to reach thousands of prospects per month without compromising your email accounts.

1) Brand-Aligned Sending Domains

Instead of sending from your main domain (e.g., abccompany.com), you get closely related domains like:

  1. teamabccompany.com
  2. abccompanyteam.com
  3. goabccompany.com
  4. getabccompany.com
  5. abccompanyhq.com

This keeps your primary business email accounts safe by isolating cold email activity from your main domain, so your normal emails never get impacted by spam filtering or reputation issues.

2) Multiple Sending Inboxes

We create professional email accounts on Google or Microsoft (e.g., john@teamabccompany.com).

Each inbox sends a small, safe number of emails per day (typically 10–20).

3) Scale Through Distribution

Instead of 1 inbox sending 2,000 emails/month, we use:

  • 10–20 inboxes
  • Each sending light daily volume
  • Resulting in thousands of emails/month with strong deliverability
