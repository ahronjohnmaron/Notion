Instagram DM Outreach for Mastermind Guest Invite

The goal is to send a minimum of 25 messages a day on instagram up to 40 Max.

Steps to Sending a Message Using Instagram:

  1. Follow the Profile, Must REPLY to TWO stories (recent or highlights) before sending icebreaker. (If you skip this step, your message has a higher percentage that it will land in "other" inbox and they will not see your message)

    Example Story Replies:

    • Yes…in full agreement
    • Woot woot! So fun!
    • You have such a beautiful family!!
    • Such a good message! Love your insight and wisdom.
    • Great tips… taking notes!
    • This is fire!!
  2. Send a DM using a Nurture Message
  3. Leave a Comment on their profile post letting them know you sent them a DM (Only if the message is not showing "seen" after 1-2 days.

    An Example Comment:

    • Love this [name]! Just dropped you a quick message when you get a sec.
    • Love your work!! Had a question for you but my message may have got buried :)

If they respond, reply back right away with the next message in your sequence. If not, Wait 24-48 hours before sending your next Nurture message.

If still no response 1 week later....

  • Send a Follow Up

When/If you get a positive response, send the invite to the event and ask if the content being covered would interest them?

  • If they reply interested, send them your invite link to the event. Along with instructions and voice note on how to successfully register.
  • Confirm they booked an interview with our community manager

If still no response 1 week after event invite...

Send a Follow Up

If still no response 1 week after event invite...

If still no response after an additional 2-3 weeks then send the invite to watch the "on demand" option.

If still no response after 3 months.

Delete the contact from your flow chat account dashboard.

Sending A Nurture + Icebreaker Message

The purpose of a nurture message is to share something about the person that is unique and original that shows you have taken time to look at their profile. (Leaving long nurture messages is too much too fast. The goal is to leave a simple, light, fun note letting them know you see them and that the work they are doing is valuable).

Choose between Pathway #1 or Pathway #2. Scroll down to see pathway #2. Test the one most in alignment with you!

DM Pathway #1:

1st Icebreaker Message:

Hi [name], I've loved following you over the last few weeks and loving your content. You're doing amazing work! Question for you, do you primarily work one-on-one or mostly with group coaching?

I know it's kinda a left field question…but I want to extend an invitation to you for something. Just want to see if it's currently aligned with your work as a coach! Looking forward to hearing back as soon as you get a moment :)

Other Possible Ice Breaker Messages

The purpose of a bridge message is to open up a conversation by asking an engaging question that leads them into a value add opportunity. This also allows you to lead by serving them so you can approach them with the right intentions.

#1: Business/Marketing Model Icebreakers

  • Hi [name], once again thanks for showing up the way you do! By chance do you happen to run challenges or do webinars?
  • Hi [name], out of curiosity… do you mainly work with clients 1-on-1 or in group setting?
  • Hi [name], by chance do you happen to run retreats? or events?
  • Hi [name], by chance do you do any live workshops or trainings?

Response to this Icebreaker:

Excellent [name], love your approach. I was asking about your model because I'm a community partner of a Mastermind and based on what you do I’d like to invite you to sit in with us. It’s $25k per student to be a part of but I’m inviting only a few other coaches to sit in on it for free. You can not buy anything and we'll be going over how to launch 100k+ workshops consistently with small groups. Would this content interest you?

Response:

Okay awesome! Here’s the link to join us:

[INSERT YOUR CUSTOM TRACKING LINK] Since you’re a guest, you’ll just need to book a quick 15-minute chat with our community manager. This is not a sales call - it’s to (1) make sure the content is relevant to you, (2) go over the ground rules of the mastermind, and (3) get you set up with your link.

Can you let me know once you book that call so I know you’re all taken care of?
