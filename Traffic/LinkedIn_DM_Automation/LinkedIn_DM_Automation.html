LinkedIn DM Automation

NOTE: We now use Ottomatey for our LinkedIn automation strategy (formally known as SocialSalesPro.) Make sure to watch the training that pairs with this!

Overview

Ottomatey is the software we use to grow an audience on LinkedIn (LI) so you can invite them to your virtual event and fill the room.

The strategy is to find targeted prospects by qualities such as job title and location so that you can send them a connection request (similar to a Facebook friend request), start a DM conversation, and lead it to a virtual event invite.

LinkedIn has roughly a limit of 1k connection requests per month and it’s time consuming to do this manually. A VA can do it, but using software is better. This is where Ottomatey comes in. It will automate those connection requests for you.

Then once you are connected, Ottomatey will then send an automated icebreaker message and allow you to queue up and send automated follow up messages if they don’t reply so that you don’t have to manually follow up. As they say, the money is in the follow up and Ottomatey does that for you automatically.

What You Will Need

To run this lead generation method, you will need the following:

  1. Ottomatey Account and License - You will want to sign up for an account and get one license for your LI profile.
  2. Your LI profile - You will want to get your profile polished up with a professional banner and intro/description written.
  3. LI Sales Navigator - You will need to purchase LI’s Sales Navigator ~$100/mo plan so that you can use its search features to find and target prospects for your business. You can get 2 months of Sales Navigator free if you have a first degree connection with another LI profile who can send you their referral link.
  4. Messaging Scripts - If you have been doing DM marketing on Instagram or Facebook already, these will come in handy for LI.
  5. VA for Inbox Management - This is optional and not necessary. Although Ottomatey no reply follow up is automated, you can hire a VA to manage the replies in your inbox and use your scripts. (that you can save inside Ottomatey to easily pull up and send) and move the prospect towards attending your virtual event.) Otherwise, be sure to set aside 15 minutes a day to go through your inbox to manage the replies you’re getting from your automated messages.
  6. Additional LI profiles if you wish to scale - Since you are limited to 1k connection requests per month on your LI profile, you will eventually want to scale and add more LI profiles. What you don’t want to do is make duplicate accounts of yourself, but instead use real existing LI profiles and convert them to ambassador profiles that will be representing your brand. We recommend Mirror Profiles where you can rent real and established LI profiles for $150/mo. Or use the LI profiles of people on your team at no upfront cost (except for paying for Sales Navigator) and compensate them every time you have an enrollment.
