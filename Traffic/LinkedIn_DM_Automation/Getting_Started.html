Getting Started

New Account

  1. Sign up with Ottomatey and then name your workspace something like your name or company.
  2. Once you’re in, click on the LinkedIn Account in the upper left corner to add your billing information and LinkedIn account. Pricing is $147/month/LI profile. For example, you create one Ottomatey account and attach two LI profiles (husband and wife), the price is $147 x 2 = $294.

Creating Your First Campaign

Here are the 💌 Templates referenced in the video

Remember: In this video you’ll see we are using SocialSalesPro, it has since been changed to Ottomatey. Same steps apply.

  • You’ll want to limit your search result links to 2,500 results/people each because that’s the max the software can do with each link in the campaign.
  • You can click the button to add more search result links to the same campaign and increase the total number of prospects in your campaign, but ideally you don’t so that you can prepare yourself in the future to scale with more profiles. For example, if you put a 100k search result link that contains your total market, you won’t have any left for a second profile in the future. Thus, you want to start getting in the habit of splitting them up now into 2k - 2.5k ish chunks to prepare yourself for future growth with more profiles.
  • You can have multiple campaigns running at the same time, but just keep in mind they’ll be sharing the total max limit of 1k connections per month so that you stay under the LinkedIn restrictions and prevent your profile from going to LinkedIn jail. So you can have 1k campaigns with 1 person in each campaign, 1 campaign with 1k people, or 1 campaign with 10k people. In all scenarios, your LI profile will still be limited to a rate of sending 1k connections per month.
  • New people get added (and removed) to your campaign automatically based on the search result because people come and go in LinkedIn. For example, you might have a search result link right now for therapists in California be 1,025 people, but then next month there will be 1,125 people because 100 more people became therapists in California on LinkedIn.
  • Where you’ll want to pay attention is if/when the New number in the campaign gets to 0. That means there’s no one left in the campaign for it to reach out to and you’ll need to add more search result links into the campaign (or start another campaign).
  • The reason why you might want to start another campaign instead adding more to the same one is that maybe you’re breaking down your campaigns by state/area and want to have specific messaging. Or maybe you’re breaking down your campaigns by job title (therapist versus psychologist) and want to have different messaging for those scenarios.
