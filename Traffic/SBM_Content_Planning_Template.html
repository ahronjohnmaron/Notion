For Management Review

This view of the database is for the manager to see all content that’s For Review and to drag it to either Revisions Needed (where the content writer revises based on the feedback) or Approved (where the appropriate person is now authorized to publish the content).

Feedback from the manager can be made by typing directly into the content, using the comments feature, or even recording a Loom video and posting the link in the comment as an easier way to explain.
