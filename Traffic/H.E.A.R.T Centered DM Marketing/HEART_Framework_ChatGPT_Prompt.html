H.E.A.R.T Framework ChatGPT Prompt

Make sure to watch the training that goes along with our H.E.A.R.T Framework. We also recommend referencing the How to Fill Events with DM Marketing with FlowChat document!

H.E.A.R.T Framework GPT Prompt:

This chat is for my (Facebook/LinkedIn/Instagram). Study my voice and the way I reach out to leads by using the scripts here:

Promise: [Enter in your promise on how you help your clients] For Example: "I help my clients unlock their ultimate confidence and breakthrough limiting beliefs so they can accomplish any goal they set"

My Voice and Tone: [Enter in your voice and tone]: For example: Friendly, authentic, warm, relatable, and intentional.

Apply this The H.E.A.R.T Framework:

1. Honor their Response - Acknowledge what they shared with warmth and care.

2. Empathize & Relate - If appropriate, connect with their experience or express understanding.

3. Ask a Thoughtful Question - and present a possibility to attend the event since they can't buy anything and it's pure value.

4. Reinforce with Encouragement - Speak life into them.

5. Transition with Possibilities - If relevant, transition to something meaningful, whether it's asking if they would be okay if I can send the link to the workshop etc.

Lead background: (Insert Lead Background)

Lead's Message: (Insert the lead's message you want to respond to)

Acknowledge the lead's message, mirror it, then integrate the message seamlessly and then ask if it's okay if I can send the link. Ensure it matches my voice.

If the output is incorrect:

"Re-do the message again. Acknowledge the lead's message, mirror it, then integrate into my script seamlessly and then ask if it's okay if I can send the link. Ensure it matches my voice."
