SBM Follow Up System

Make sure to check out the training that goes with this!

SBM Follow Up System of Sequences:

  1. They Opt-IN But Did Not Book a Pre-Interview Call
  2. They Opt-in and Booked Pre-Interview but Did Not Show Up
  3. They Got Rescheduled and Still Didn’t Show Up to Pre-Interview
  4. They Got Scheduled for The Event But Did Not Show Up to Feedback
  5. They Came to the Event and showed up to Feedback Call but Did Not See the Offer (The Enroller)
  6. They were confirmed for the event but did not show to the event and still have a feedback call scheduled.
  7. They came to the event, came to the feedback call, did not enroll but wants to enroll in the near future.
  • The Follow Up + Pre-Interview

Scenario 1: Opted-in But Did Not Book A Call

Order:

  1. Call
  2. If no answer, leave a voicemail
  3. Text

1. Call Script

“Hey is this [NAME]?”

Response: Yes.

“Hey [NAME], this is Thomas calling on behalf of the Smart Business Mastermind. You just requested access to Adam Flores' training on How to launch $100k+ Workshops with Small Groups and AI.

I just wanted to call and confirm you're still interest in sitting in with us. Do you remember registering for it?

If yes, if you have a few minutes now we can have a quick chat and I can help get you set up?”

If yes, proceed to next step: The follow up pre-interview

  • If no, share the headline of the event and re-ask if they are interested in this topic?
  • If not, interested try and ask them what their goals are to create an interest.
  • If still no signs of interest, exit the call.

B. Text Script

“Hello [NAME],

Recently, you requested access to Adam Flores' training on How to launch $100k+ Workshops with Small Groups and AI.

Can you please confirm or decline your interest?”

Response: Yes.

After 1 Business Day:

If no response, follow up next day and call again. If no answer, send text:

“Hey [NAME], just checking to see if you got my text yesterday?”

Note: If yes, follow the next step.

After 2 Business Days:

If no response, follow up next day and call again. If no answer, send text:

“Hey [NAME], just following up. Just wanted to confirm if you still plan on joining us?”

Note: If yes, follow the next step.

After 3 Business Days

If no response, follow up next day and call again. If no answer, send text:

“Hey [NAME], last time following up, just wanted to check in and see if you are still interested in joining us?”

Note: If yes, follow the next step

If They Confirm Interest (Via Text) to the Event, then Send This Text:

“Great.

Just need to schedule a 15 minute call with our community manager [NAME]. She'll give you all the details and information you need to know going into the training.

She has tomorrow at 1pm or 2:30pm Eastern available. Does this work for you?

Or,

If you have a few minutes now we can have a quick chat and I can help get you set up?” (This is if they are able to conduct the pre-interview themselves.)

Scenario 3: They Got Rescheduled and Still Didn’t Show Up to Pre-Interview

Text Script:

“Hey [NAME], I see you weren’t able to attend your rescheduled call with “name of interviewer”, she has [date and time] / [time zone] tomorrow. Do any of these times work for you?”

  • Goal of Call: Do the pre-interview yourself. If they don’t have time, get them back into the interviewers calendar.

Follow Up Call + Pre-Interview:

Tone: Casual

Call Script:

“Hey this is Thomas with the Smart Business Mastermind”. I see here you had a call scheduled with my colleague Mariah yesterday that you were unable to attend. So I just figured I’d call to see if you have a few minutes now and we can have a quick chat over the phone if that works for you.”

Prospect: Sure

“Great! If you don’t mind, can you start off by sharing what you do and how you help your clients?”

Prospect Answers: I help my clients with mindset breakthrough.

That’s great! If you don’t mind how many clients are you currently working with and what does that financial investment look like for your services?

Prospect: I currently have 5 clients, and I charge $1500 each.

Do you work in groups, individually, a combination of both?

Prospect: I have both.

Now, do you do this full time, or is this side work for you and you want to be full time eventually?

Prospect: I do work but want this to be full time.

What field do you work in? Is it a similar field?

Based on what you shared with me, we have a training called:

1    From Setup to Sold Out:  How to Hit Consistent 10-30k per month

2     How to Launch 100k virtual events with small groups and AI

I believe it would be a fit for you.

Adam is the top business strategist in the world for coaches and consultants, as he has helped some of the top entrepreneurs in this space with their marketing and offers. Bye.”

Scenario 4: They Came to the Event But Did Not Show Up to Feedback

This is the job of the enroller: The enroller will text them, call them and email them to try and get them back on the books.

If they can't reach them after 2-3 days, they may send the names over to follow up to continue trying to get ahold of them.

Scenario 5: They Came to the Event and showed up to Feedback Call but Did Not See the Offer (The Enroller)

This is the job of the enroller: The enroller will text them, call them and email them to try and get them back on the books.

If they did not see the offer, typically the enroller will reengage them, send them the recording of the offer, and book another feedback call after the person give the time they can watch the 30 min offer video.

Scenario 6: They were confirmed for the event but did not show to the event and still have a feedback call scheduled.

This is originally the job of the pre-interviewer. They are on during the event texting anyone who is not there that said they would be. If late response, we will engage them into our on-demand experience if they miss the event.

After the event is over, this now becomes the job of the enroller: The enroller will text them, call them and email them before their feedback call date. Their goal is to still keep the feedback call to get them into the ondemand experience of the event and reschedule another feedback call.

Scenario 7: They came to the event, came to the feedback call, did not enroll but wants to enroll in the near future.

This is the job of the enroller: The enroller will mark follow up dates in the calendar. They will text them, call them and email them to try and get them back on the books.

Additional Notes:

Event 1: For newbie coaches or agency services or aspiring coaches/agency services

Always on Second Monday of Month (Except Nov & Dec) at 2pm pst -5pm pst.

Called: Take “Your Offer from Setup to Sold Out:  How to Hit Consistent 10-30k months”

  • Key language: Map out their offer: (Structure it, Price it, How do they deliver it, and how do they market it so they can get more clients and create consistent 10-30k months.)
  • In addition: We can allow those who want to learn.

Event 2: For coaches, consultants or agency owners who already have experience.

Always on Third Monday of Month (Except Nov & Dec) at 2pm pst -6pm pst.

Key things to look for: (Already have clients. Have an offer price of $2k-$25k+). They already manage clients and want to grow past 20k, 50k, or 100k months).

Title: “How to Launch 100k virtual events with small groups and AI”.

  • Key Language: We are going to show you, how we structure our events, how we fill our events, how to optimize your offer and how to pitch your offer so you can scale with many at once. We also cover the latest AI strategies which Adam will share with you in the event.
  • Edification: Top business strategist in the world for coaches and consultants, as he has helped some of the top entrepreneurs in this space with their marketing and offers. People say he is one of the best teachers and has an amazing gift to help you increase your growth and people rave about his training so I think you will be blown away.
  • Selling points: They can not buy anything during the event. Why? To create a safe room, receive content and not worry about it being one big sales pitch!

4 Rules to Attend:

  1. They have to agree that they are clear on what we are teaching and be excited to learn the topic.
  2. We ask that you are available to stay for the entire event because Adam wants you to walk away with transformation not just bits of information.
  3. We ask that you keep your camera on during the event. Why? It’s how we keep our rooms safe.
  4. In exchange for the content, all we ask for is your feedback (30 min call). We value your insight and this how we continue to make our trainings better. This is also your opportunity to learn about further mentorship if you want to take next steps.

If they are UNABLE TO ATTEND:

  1. We can give them the “On Demand” 2.5 hours of training. If they choose this option they need to book a feedback call, require they complete it and communicate with our community manager Amanda through the experience. Let them know they will be put in a text thread and ask them if they agree to finish and respond to our community manager in exchange for the training.
  2. We book them to attend the next live workshop the following month.
