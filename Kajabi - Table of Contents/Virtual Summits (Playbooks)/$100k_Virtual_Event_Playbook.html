$100k Virtual Event Playbook

$100k Virtual Event Playbook - How to put together and run a free multi-day training event online that you'll be inviting your prospects to so that you can deliver a bunch of value and pitch your offer. VIP Worksheet attached on every training.

  • Day 1: Housekeeping - How to open up an event with your values and beliefs, introducing people on your team, sharing your story/journey, getting the event started, breakout rooms for participants to meet each other.
  • Day 1: Event Calculator, Revenue Goals, Vision - Learn how to use our Event Calculator to see how many clients you need to hit your income goals, get clear on what outcome you want your clients to have, what culture you want to create, etc. Document attached.
  • Day 1: Structuring the Offer for Your Event - Learn how to get clear and structure your offer for your events.
  • Bonus: How to Set Up Headlines For Your Virtual Event - How to write headlines for the event registration webpage for your virtual event. Document attached.
  • Day 2: Gate, Q&A, Speakers - The pre-event interview calls, how to set up the event registration webpages if you're not doing the pre-event interview calls, and how to have speakers at your event.
  • Day 2: Derron Walker Panelist - Hear from Derron and learn more about his personal journey and how he’s been successful hitting over 80k from his 3 Day event!
  • Day 2: Organization of Event - Learn how to get clear on your content to lead an impactful and organized event.
  • Day 3: Leading with Transformation - Learn how to lead your events with transformation vs information.
  • Day 3: Delivering Your Offer and Q&A - Learn how to deliver your offer during your event, plus Q&A.
  • Day 3: Mutual Encouragement and Closing - Hear members of SBM go around and give encouragement to one another.
  • Pre-Interview Role Play -Adam and Kristy, one of our pre-enrollment specialists, role play a pre-interview call, using the script that you can also share with your team!
  • Bonus: How Marcea Enrolls Clients During Her Pre-Interview Process - Marcea shares her process and ways to make sales during the pre-interview process.
  • Bonus: Event Tracker Template - Track where your lead sources are coming from. Template attached.
  • Bonus: How to Set Up Your Agenda Outline for Your Virtual Event - Learn how to create an agenda outline for your virtual event.
