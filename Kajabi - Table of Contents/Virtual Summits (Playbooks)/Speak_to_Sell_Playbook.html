Speak to Sell Playbook

Speak to Sell Playbook - Learn how to get paid to speak at events, but mostly to learn how to approach other people who have your target audience with an offer to do a short and valuable 1-hour free training so that you can promote and invite them to your free multi-day virtual event.

  • Day 1: Housekeeping & Intro on How to Get the Most Out of this Event! - This training provides an introduction to maximizing the benefits of the 'Speak to Sell' playbook.
  • Day 1: Restructuring Time to Increase Your Earning Power. - Learn how to maximize your time so you can bring in more revenue.
  • Day 1: How the "Pay to Play" Speaking Business Works and How to Book Gigs Now! - This training will cover the ins and outs of the 'Pay to Play' speaking industry and provide actionable strategies to start booking gigs immediately.
  • Day 1: How to Come Up with Your "Speak to Sell" Topics! - Learn the process on how to create your Speak to Sell topics.
  • Day 1: 30 Minute "Speak to Sell" Framework - Learn our Speak to Sell framework we use at SBM. Document attached.
  • Day 2: Building Out Content for Your "Speak to Sell" Talk - Learn how to build content for your Speak to Sell talk. Document attached.
  • Day 2: Laurie-Ann Murabito: How to Get "Fee Paid" Speaking Engagements - Learn how to find and get booked to speak in front of your ideal clients with Laurie-Ann.
  • Day 2: Laurie-Ann Murabito Q&A Breakout - Q&A with Laurie-Ann
  • Day 2: Mastering the "Call-to-Action" to Your Talk! - Learn how to master and the importance the Call-to-Action to your talk.
  • Day 2: Speaker Swipe File & Building Your Top 25 Speaker List - This training focuses on creating a speaker swipe file and building a top 25 list of influential speakers. Documents attached.
  • Day 3: Rory Vaden Interview: The Guy Behind the TOP 1% of Speakers & Influencers in the World! - This training features an interview with Rory Vaden, highlighting insights from the top 1% of speakers and influencers worldwide. PDF & document attached.
  • Day 3: Utilizing VA's to Land Speaking Gigs to Leverage Your Time! - This training teaches how to use virtual assistants to secure speaking gigs and maximize your time efficiency.
